<p>

<p>
  <br />This works really well in WPF, but it doesn’t work in Silverlight.  Why?  Because the Silverlight runtime applies a SecurityCriticalAttribute to the <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/deysbxxw(VS.95).aspx" target="_blank">StackFrame</a> class, restricting its use to the Silverlight framework.  Understandable, but that means I cannot pull off RaisePropertyChanged() inside a Silverlight application.  Hard-coded strings it is.</p>
