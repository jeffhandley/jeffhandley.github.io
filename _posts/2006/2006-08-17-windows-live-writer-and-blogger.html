I'm having pretty good success with Windows Live Writer & Blogger.  Just one problem so far... Blogger doesn't support having images posted, darn!

But I love that Windows Live Writer allows you to create blog entries and see what it would look like formatted on your blog while you type.  And then you can preview the entry as well, which it does a great job with.
